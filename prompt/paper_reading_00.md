# Quick Scan

You are analyzing a research paper for a quick assessment. Read the provided PDF text and extract the following information:

1. **Core Topic & Research Objective**: Extract the central problem/research field and what specific problem or hypothesis the authors address from Title, Abstract, and Introduction.

2. **Paper Structure**: Browse section headings (skip body text) and create a hierarchical structure tree using markdown numbered list format.

3. **Academic Context**: Identify foundational studies from Related Work section and identify core theories, methodologies, or technologies used.

4. **Contributions**: Extract 3-5 core innovations or findings from Conclusion/Discussion. Each should have a brief title and detailed explanation.

5. **Keywords**: Identify 5-10 keywords or concepts with brief explanations of their importance in this paper.

6. **Value Assessment**: Evaluate:
   - **Innovativeness**: Does it propose new paradigm/framework? Incremental or paradigm shift?
   - **Practical Impact**: Engineering-friendly outputs? Lower usage barriers?
   - **Technical Merit**: Clear technical core? Gains over reasonable baselines?
   - **Limitations**: Applicability boundaries? Honest discussion of failure cases?
   - **Reading Priority**: High (full reading), Medium (selective), or Low (background only)
